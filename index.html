About Me

Hi! I'm Emma. I am a native Angeleno. I am married with four grown children, a wonderful son-in-law, and two beautiful granddaughters.

I’ve been a paralegal for 34 years (working at law firms in both the San Fernando Valley and Los Angeles). During this time, I observed the needs of those that did not need an attorney, couldn’t afford an attorney, or didn’t want an attorney. I decided I could offer them an alternative that would save them both time and money.

As a result, I opened my office in an effort to help people that were seeking assistance without having to dig deep into their pockets and who wanted someone who is genuinely concerned in assisting them in an effort to resolve their matter expeditiously and amicably. I’m an affordable alternative for people who need legal document preparation (and don’t feel that a law firm is the way to go). I was a volunteer CASA advocate and know how necessary affordable legal assistance is to many in the community I live in and in neighboring communities.
